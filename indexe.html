LEARN ALL ABOUT HORSES

If you thought until now that riding a horse is the only thing you can learn with us, then we are changing your mind...

APPROACHING THE HORSE

One of the very important things that is often neglected is just getting to know and getting close to the horse. The first contact that precedes the actual mounting of the horse is decisive in its obedience. In order to succeed in riding, it is necessary to know how to communicate with a horse, i.e. body language, giving signals to each other and recognizing them.

HORSE MAINTENANCE

Not only can you gain knowledge and experience in approaching and riding horses, but you can also learn everything about the maintenance and care of the horse itself. We make you independent in breeding and the whole discipline with horses. With us, you can learn how to bathe, brush, clean hooves, saddle, properly adjust the reins and many other things that are necessary in working with a horse.

HORSE RIDING

And finally the most interesting part HORSE RIDING in different ways with different techniques such as: walk, trot, easy gallop, canter, run, fast riding, jumping and crossing obstacles. You can learn all that and much more with us.
